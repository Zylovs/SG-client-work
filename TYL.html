Where do I even start ?

Back in September of 2021 I was in an RP server, first one I ever joined too. I had already been in there for a month so I was getting the gist of what RP is all about and what's expected. During that small amount of time I met some people that I now consider my best friends. I saw what an amazing group of people was involved in this community, but there was something that just wasn't there for me. That one thing is passion for the people. When it comes to building a community the #1 thing that matters most, is all of you. I remember back in September briefly mentioning to one whom I now consider my best friend Boon that I was going to make my own server. We both were excited and threw out crazy ideas, but it never really went past that. A couple more months past and in December Habibi purchased a game host for me to give it a shot, but man did I have NO clue how hard it was to start a FiveM server. I definitely have more respect for FiveM devs now than I did back then. After messing around for a couple weeks I honestly gave up. It was far to great a challenge for one man to tackle so I just went back to my normal RP routine. As more time went on I saw these people I had met start to dwindle down to just a couple. People were leaving to go to other community's because they no longer felt heard. They felt as if they had no voice and they didn't matter. That's when I knew that it was time. I had to get some amazing like-minded people together with the same views and visions and really kick this thing off. So I spoke with Wearzy, Campbell, Greg, and Gaines to see what they thought of the idea. As you all see now, they were absolutely stoked. So we kick started this thing we call Summit Gaming. Now that the rambling is over I can get to what this is all about. This is the Founders page. Any name you see above this are the people that were here from day 1, the people that helped kickstart this entire thing. The people that I honestly can't thank enough. Even all of you, the community members. Thank you for giving us all of these amazing ideas because without those we would have a basic qb-core server. Without you guys there would be no RP. There wouldn't be a foundation to build upon. I truly can't thank you people enough. I promise so long as Summit Gaming is here we will always listen and value your opinions and suggestions. We will ALWAYS listen even if you feel like it's no big deal. No matter what, this is OUR community.
